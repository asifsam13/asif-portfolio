Alpine Financial Instagram Reels

Making a "dry" industry survive the first 3 seconds.

Situation
A US-based insurance and financial-services brand whose expertise didn't translate to social; they needed consistent, high-retention short-form content that didn't look like a corporate ad.
Task
End-to-end editing of two Reels per week: raw talking-head and B-roll in, scroll-stopping platform-native assets out.
Action
Dynamic pacing, bold typography, and strategic sound design in Premiere Pro and CapCut Pro: trend-aware editing on top of a strictly professional brand identity, engineered to hold viewers past the 3-second mark.
Result
A library of on-brand, high-performing Reels; the client renewed week over week and closed the contract at a perfect 5.0.
