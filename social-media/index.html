CASE STUDIES

How the growth happened

01

Bookstagram 0 to 20K

A highly competitive niche, cracked without a single ad.

Situation
A brand-new account in the crowded bookish niche: zero followers, zero history, and an audience famously loyal to established creators.
Task
Build a real, engaged audience organically; no paid promotion, no follow-for-follow tricks.
Action
Carousel-first content system (reviews, standout quotes, consistent branding), narrative flow engineered for saves and shares, and performance data fed back into the strategy weekly.
Result
20K followers in under 10 months, with the account becoming a recognized voice in its niche.
02

Engagement Tripled in 100 Days

Same account, same audience, three times the interaction.

Situation
An existing account with moderate engagement that had plateaued; content was going out but conversations weren't happening.
Task
Lift the interaction rate meaningfully without changing the brand's voice or buying reach.
Action
Strategic content optimization: hook-led captions, engagement-bait-free prompts that people actually answer, community management with fast, human replies, and doubling down on the formats the data favored.
Result
Interaction rate tripled within 100 days, and the lift held after the sprint ended.
03

Curated By Emiel Luxury Antiques

A premium brand presence built from zero.

Situation
A new Scottish antique dealership needed a premium social presence from scratch, aimed at a narrow, luxury-focused audience.
Task
Develop the brand voice, a targeted content calendar, and an engagement strategy built on heritage storytelling instead of standard product posts.
Action
Deep-dive market research, a detailed content roadmap around each piece's history, and a direct outreach playbook targeting local interior design firms.
Result
An elegant, actionable launch foundation with the exact cost structure and clarity the client lacked; rated 5.0.
