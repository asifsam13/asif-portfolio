CASE STUDIES

How the growth happened

01

Bookstagram 0 to 20K

A highly competitive niche, cracked without a single ad.

The problem
A brand-new account in the crowded bookish niche: zero followers, zero history, and an audience famously loyal to established creators. It needed a real, engaged audience with no paid promotion and no follow-for-follow tricks.
What I did
A carousel-first content system (reviews, standout quotes, consistent branding), narrative flow built for saves and shares, and performance data fed back into the strategy weekly.
The result
20K followers in under 10 months, and the account became a recognized voice in its niche.
02

Engagement Tripled in 100 Days

Same account, same audience, three times the interaction.

The problem
An existing account with moderate engagement that had plateaued. Content was going out but conversations weren't happening, and the interaction rate needed to rise without buying reach.
What I did
Hook-led captions, prompts people actually answer, fast human replies, and doubling down on the formats the data favored.
The result
Interaction rate tripled within 100 days, and the lift held after the sprint ended.
03

Curated By Emiel Luxury Antiques

A premium brand presence built from zero.

The problem
A new Scottish antique dealership needed a premium social presence from scratch: brand voice, content calendar, and an engagement strategy for a narrow, luxury-focused audience.
What I did
Deep market research, a content roadmap built around each piece's history, and a direct outreach playbook targeting local interior design firms.
The result
An actionable launch foundation with the exact cost structure and clarity the client lacked; rated 5.0.
